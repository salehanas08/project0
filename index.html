Welcome to the School Application Portal

The school wishes to invite application into both Junior and Senior High school classes for the 2020 Academic Session. Interested applicants should note that application will be open till 29th July, 2020. Before proceeding with the application it is however, highly recommended that the guidliness and details about the eligibility for the application be read and understood. Kindly provide the necessary details with valid phone number to proceed with the application.

Requirements.
  • A recent passport of not later than 3 weeks.
  • Valid parent/guardian phone number
  • Birth and indigen Certificates for upload
  • First School leaving certificate (FSLC) for upload
Eligibility.
  • Candidate must have his Primary school leaving certificates handy.
  • Candidate seeking for admission into senior section must have passed the BECE examination.
  • Student on transfer must upload their transfer letter and state reason for transfer.
